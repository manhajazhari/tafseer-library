SURAH AL-ASR · ٱلْعَصْر

The Oath by Time — The Formula for Success

Three short verses, yet Imām al-Shāfiʿī said: "If people pondered only this Surah, it would be enough for them." Allah swears by Time itself that every human being is in loss — except those who unite four things: true faith, righteous deeds, mutual exhortation to truth, and mutual exhortation to patience. The universal formula for a life that is not wasted.
