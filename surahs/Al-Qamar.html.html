SURAH AL-QAMAR · ٱلْقَمَر

The Moon — Splitting Sign, Destroyed Nations, and the Seat of Truth

Surah Al-Qamar is a Makkan surah of 55 verses. It opens with the imminent Hour and the literal splitting of the moon — the miracle Quraysh witnessed at Mina. It marches through five destroyed nations (Nuh, 'Aad, Thamud, Lut, Pharaoh), each closed with the refrain "and We have made the Qur'an easy for remembrance — so is there any who will remember?" It contains a Makkan prophecy of the defeat at Badr, one of the Qur'an's foundational Qadar verses, and closes with the believer "in a seat of truth, near a Sovereign Most Powerful."
