SURAH AL-LAYL · ٱللَّيْل

By the Night! — Two Paths, Two Destinies

Allah swears by the night when it covers, the day when it shines, and the creation of male and female — to reveal a profound truth: human efforts are shattā (different and diverse). Some give and fear Allah; others hoard and turn away. Two souls, two paths, two destinies. Revealed about Abu Bakr رضي الله عنه as he spent his fortune to free the tortured slave Bilal, Surah Al-Layl is the Quran's most beautiful portrait of generosity versus stinginess.
