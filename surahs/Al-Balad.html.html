SURAH AL-BALAD · ٱلْبَلَد

No! I Swear By This City — The Steep Path of Real Faith

Allah swears by the sacred city of Makkah, by the father and what he begot, and reveals a powerful truth: humanity was created in toil and struggle. The arrogant person boasts of wasted wealth and thinks no one sees him — but Allah gave him eyes, a tongue, and lips, and guided him to two clear paths. Yet he refuses to climb al-ʿAqabah — the steep ascent: freeing a slave, feeding the orphan and the poor, and being among those who believe and counsel each other to patience and mercy. This is the path of the People of the Right; rejecting it is the path of the People of the Left, trapped in fire.
