SURAH AL-INSHIQAQ · ٱلِانْشِقَاق

When the Sky Is Split Open — The Toiling Journey to Allah

When the sky is split asunder, the earth is stretched flat and casts out all it holds — every soul stands before its Lord. Surah Al-Inshiqaq portrays humanity as a traveler toiling toward Allah, and unveils two destinies: the one who receives his record in his right hand and returns to his family rejoicing, and the one who receives it behind his back and calls out for destruction. It closes with glad tidings for the believers and a warning to the deniers: "You will surely pass on from one stage to another."
