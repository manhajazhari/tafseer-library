SURAH ASH-SHAMS · ٱلشَّمْس

The Sun and the Soul

Allah swears by eleven of His greatest creations — the sun, the moon, the day, the night, the sky, the earth, and the human soul — to announce one of the most profound truths in the Qur'an: success belongs to those who purify their souls, and ruin awaits those who corrupt them. The Surah then warns with the story of Thamud, the people of Prophet Salih ﷺ, who rejected the truth, slaughtered the she-camel, and were destroyed in a single night.
