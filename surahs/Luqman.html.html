SURAH LUQMAN · لُقْمَان

Luqman — The Wisdom of a Father to His Son

Surah Luqman is a Makkan surah of 34 verses, named after the wise servant of Allah Luqman al-Hakeem. It opens with the gift of wisdom granted to him, then preserves his beautiful, tender advice to his son: do not associate partners with Allah, honour your parents, establish the prayer, enjoin the good and forbid the evil, be patient, do not turn your cheek away from people in arrogance, and lower your voice. Around these wasaaya the surah weaves the proofs of Allah's oneness in creation, refutes blind imitation of the fathers, and seals with the five keys of the unseen known only to Allah.
