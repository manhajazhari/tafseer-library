SURAH AL-HASHR · ٱلْحَشْر

The Gathering — Bani an-Nadir, the Fay', the Hypocrites, and the Most Beautiful Names

Surah Al-Hashr is a Madani surah of 24 verses on the exile of Bani an-Nadir, the verses of fay', the hypocrites who promised but never delivered, the parable of Shaytan, the command of self-accounting, and the closing of eleven divine names recited every evening.
