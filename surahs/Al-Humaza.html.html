SURAH AL-HUMAZAH · ٱلْهُمَزَة

Wail! — The Warning to Every Slanderer

In the bustling markets and grand assemblies of Makkah, the wealthy elite sat on elevated seats, looking down at those beneath them. With a wink here and a whisper there, they tore apart reputations. With gold piled in their vaults, they believed themselves immortal. Then came a thunderous warning from the heavens — a single word that shattered their delusion: "Wail!" — Destruction! Surah Al-Humazah is a divine mirror held up to every age, exposing the emptiness of wealth without faith and the poison of a tongue unchecked.
