SURAH AL-MA'OON · ٱلْمَاعُون

The Small Kindnesses — A Surah That Exposes Hypocrisy and Honours the Orphan

Seven verses of thunderous rebuke — addressed to a people who prayed with their bodies but denied the Day of Judgment with their hearts, who repelled orphans and neglected the poor, whose worship was theatrical display, and who refused even the smallest neighbourly kindness. Surah Al-Ma'oon is the Quran's surgical scalpel against performative religion: it separates real faith from its hollow imitation.
