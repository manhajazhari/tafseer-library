SURAH AL-MUDDATHTHIR · ٱلْمُدَّثِّر

The Cloaked One — Rise and Warn

Surah Al-Muddaththir is one of the earliest Makkan surahs revealed to the Prophet ﷺ after the pause in revelation. It opens with the gentle yet urgent call: "O you who are cloaked — rise and warn!" — launching the mission of prophethood. The surah then unveils the trumpet of the Last Day, the punishment of the haughty denier (Al-Walid ibn al-Mughira), a chilling description of Saqar (Hell) with its nineteen keepers, and finally a piercing dialogue between the people of Paradise and the criminals: "What led you into Saqar?" — they answer: "We were not of those who prayed."
