SURAH AL-KAFIROON · ٱلْكَافِرُون

Al-Kafiroon — A Declaration of Pure Tawheed

Surah Al-Kafiroon — "The Disbelievers" — is the Prophet ﷺ's clear answer to the polytheists of Quraysh when they proposed that he worship their idols for a year if they would worship Allah for a year. The surah draws an unbreakable line between the worship of the One True God and the worship of anything else. It teaches absolute sincerity (ikhlāṣ), peaceful disassociation from shirk, and the right of the believer to hold his religion without compromise.
