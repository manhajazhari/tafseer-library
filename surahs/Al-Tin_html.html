SURAH AT-TEEN · ٱلتِّينِ

The Fig — Human Honour & the Final Judgment

Surah At-Teen opens with a solemn oath by four sacred symbols — the fig, the olive, Mount Sinai, and the City of Security — to affirm the noblest truth about humanity: Allah created the human being in the finest form. Then comes the warning: whoever turns away from faith and good deeds falls to the lowest of the low. The surah closes with a decisive question — is Allah not the most just of judges? A Makkan surah that addresses human dignity, accountability, and the justice of the Creator.
