SURAH AL-FATIHA · ٱلْفَاتِحَة

The Opening — A Complete Conversation with Allah

Surah Al-Fatiha is the greatest surah in the Quran — recited in every unit of every prayer, seventeen times each day. It is a dialogue between the servant and his Lord: Allah praises Himself through our tongues, and we turn to Him alone in worship and in need. It maps the three paths of humanity: those who receive Allah's blessings, those who earn His anger, and those who go astray — and teaches us to ask for the first every single day.
