SURAH AL-QIYAMAH · ٱلْقِيَامَة

The Resurrection — Proof, Scene, and Reckoning

Surah Al-Qiyamah is an early Makkan surah whose central mission is to establish the reality of bodily resurrection. It opens with two divine oaths — by the Day of Resurrection itself and by the self-reproaching soul — refuting the disbeliever who thought his bones could never be rejoined. It paints the cosmic scene of that Day, contrasts radiant faces gazing at their Lord with grim faces expecting a back-breaking blow, pauses on the soul that has reached the collar-bones, rebukes the one who neither believed nor prayed, and closes with the conclusive proof from man's own origin: a drop of fluid — therefore He who began you can certainly bring you back.
