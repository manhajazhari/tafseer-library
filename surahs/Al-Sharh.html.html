SURAH AL-SHARH · ٱلشَّرْح

Did We Not Expand — Relief After Every Hardship

Revealed as a direct consolation to the Prophet ﷺ in times of burden, Surah Al-Sharh asks three rhetorical questions — Did We not expand your breast? Did We not remove your burden? Did We not raise your mention? — and then promises twice that with every hardship comes ease. It closes with a timeless directive: "When you are free, strive — and to your Lord direct your longing."
