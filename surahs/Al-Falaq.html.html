SURAH AL-FALAQ · ٱلْفَلَق

Al-Falaq — Refuge in the Lord of the Dawn

Surah Al-Falaq teaches the believer to seek refuge in Allah — the Lord who splits dawn from night — against every harm in creation, against the dangers of darkness, against sorcery, and against envy. It is one of the two Mu'awwidhatayn, recited by the Prophet ﷺ for protection morning and evening, after every prayer, and at the moment he was bewitched.
