SURAH AL-MULK · ٱلْمُلْك

The Dominion — All Sovereignty Belongs to Allah

Surah Al-Mulk is the Makkan surah that opens with the blessed declaration that dominion belongs to Allah alone. The Prophet ﷺ called it Al-Maani'ah and Al-Munjiyah — the surah that pleads for its companion and saves him from the punishment of the grave. It walks the reader through the seven heavens, the splendor of the night sky, the soaring birds, and the depths of Hell — calling the heart to reflect on the One who created death and life to test who is best in deed.
