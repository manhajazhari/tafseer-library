SURAH QURAYSH · قُرَيْش

Ilaf Quraysh — A Gift of Safety and Sustenance

In the heart of the Arabian Peninsula stood Makkah — a barren valley with no rivers, no fields, no protection but the sanctuary of the Ka'bah. Yet a small tribe, the Quraysh, flourished there. Why? Because Allah granted them Ilaf — a sacred covenant of safety and brotherhood that allowed their caravans to travel south to Yemen in winter and north to Sham in summer, untouched by the raids that destroyed other tribes. Surah Quraysh is a divine reminder: every blessing you enjoy — food, safety, family — is a direct gift from the Lord of this House. The only fitting response is worship.
