SURAH AL-BUROOJ · ٱلْبُرُوج

By the Sky Full of Constellations! — Faith That Did Not Bend

Allah swears by the sky filled with great constellations, by the Promised Day, and by the witness and the witnessed — then He tells the story of Aṣḥāb al-Ukhdūd, the People of the Trench: a tyrant king dug a fiery pit and threw believers into it for one reason — they said, "Our Lord is Allah." Surah Al-Burooj is the Quran's most powerful tribute to steadfast faith and a stark warning to every persecutor that Allah's grip is severe and His knowledge encompasses all.
