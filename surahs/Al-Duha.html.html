SURAH AD-DUHA · ٱلضُّحَىٰ

The Morning After the Silence

For a period, the revelation paused. The Prophet ﷺ was deeply distressed, wondering if his Lord was displeased with him — and the disbelievers mocked, claiming his Lord had abandoned him. Then Allah sent down Ad-Duha: an oath by the brightness of morning and the stillness of night, a tender promise that He had neither left him nor was angry — and that what is coming is far better than what has passed.
