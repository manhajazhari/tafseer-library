SURAH AL-MUNAFIQUN · ٱلْمُنَافِقُون

The Hypocrites — Tongues That Say What Hearts Deny

Surah Al-Munafiqun is a Madinan surah revealed on the expedition of Banu al-Mustaliq. It unmasks ‘Abdullah ibn Ubayy and the hypocrites who pretended faith with their tongues while their hearts denied it. The surah exposes their false oaths, their hollow bodies and impressive speech, their plot to starve the Muhajirun, and their boast that the ‘noble’ would expel the ‘humbled’ from Madinah — answered by Allah: ‘To Allah belongs all honour, and to His Messenger, and to the believers.’ It closes with a warning to the believers themselves: do not let wealth or children distract you from the remembrance of Allah — and spend before death arrives and the chance is gone forever.
