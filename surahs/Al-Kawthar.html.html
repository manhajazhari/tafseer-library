SURAH AL-KAWTHAR · ٱلْكَوْثَر

The Abundance — A Gift Greater Than Any Loss

In Makkah, after the death of the Prophet's beloved infant son, the enemies of Islam — led by Al-'As ibn Wa'il — sneered that Muhammad ﷺ was now "abtar": cut off, with no male heir to carry his name. In that moment of grief and mockery, the heavens answered. Allah revealed the shortest surah in the Qur'an — only three ayat — yet it crushed their taunt and crowned His Messenger with an everlasting gift: Al-Kawthar, the river of abundance in Paradise, and a legacy that would echo across every generation. Surah Al-Kawthar is the promise that with Allah, no believer is ever truly cut off.
