SURAT AL-MUZZAMMIL · ٱلْمُزَّمِّل

The Wrapped One — Stand the Night, Carry the Heavy Word

Surat Al-Muzzammil is a Makkan surah of 20 verses, revealed in the earliest days of the Prophet's ﷺ mission. After the first descent of revelation in Hira, the Prophet ﷺ came home shaking and asked to be wrapped (zammilooni). Allah called him by his very state — 'O you wrapped' — and prescribed the first training program of Islam: stand the night, recite the Qur'an with measured tarteel, prepare for a heavy word, devote yourself to Allah, withdraw beautifully from the deniers, and trust the Lord of the east and the west. The surah closes with the most merciful concession: recite what is easy from the Qur'an, establish the prayer, give zakah, lend Allah a goodly loan, and seek His forgiveness.
