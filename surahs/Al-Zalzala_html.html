SURAH AL-ZALZALAH · ٱلزَّلْزَلَة

The Earthquake — When the Earth Speaks and Every Atom Witnesses

Eight verses of cosmic terror and eternal justice — a surah that compresses the entire Day of Judgment into a single earthquake. When the earth is shaken with its final shaking, it will expel its dead and bear witness to every deed buried within it. Man will ask in terror, 'What is wrong with her?' On that Day, the earth will narrate its news — because your Lord has inspired it to speak. This is the surah that terrifies the oppressor and consoles the oppressed: even an atom's weight of good or evil will be seen.
