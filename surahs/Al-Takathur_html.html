SURAH AT-TAKATHUR · ٱلتَّكَاثُر

At-Takathur — A Warning Against Rivalry for Worldly Gain

Surah At-Takathur — "The Rivalry in Worldly Increase" — is a powerful Makkan warning that human beings are easily distracted by competition over wealth, status and possessions until death overtakes them. The surah shakes the heart with a repeated "Kallā" ("Nay!"), reminds the reader of the visit to the graves, the certain sight of the Hellfire, and the questioning on the Day of Judgement about every blessing Allah granted.
