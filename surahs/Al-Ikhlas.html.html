Al-Ikhlas — The Constitution of Tawheed

Surah Al-Ikhlas is the heart of belief in Allah. In four short verses it establishes who Allah truly is: One without partner, eternally needed by all yet needing none, without origin or offspring, resembling nothing. The Prophet ﷺ taught that it equals a third of the Quran — because it perfects the creed upon which the whole religion stands.
