SURAH AL-FIL · ٱلْفِيل

The Elephant That Refused to March

In the year the Prophet ﷺ was born, an Abyssinian governor named Abrahah marched a massive army — led by war elephants — towards Makkah to destroy the Ka'bah. What happened next was so extraordinary that an entire age was named after it: 'Aam al-Fil, the Year of the Elephant. Allah preserved His sacred House without a single human soldier defending it. This Surah is the divine eyewitness report.
