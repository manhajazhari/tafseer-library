SURAH AL-MASAD · ٱلْمَسَد

Perished Are the Hands of Abu Lahab

When the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ first proclaimed Islam openly on Mount Safa, his own uncle — Abu Lahab — answered the call to truth with a curse: "Tabban laka!" ("May you perish!"). Allah responded with this short, devastating Surah, naming Abu Lahab and his wife by description, and prophesying their painful end. Surah Al-Masad stands as a timeless warning against arrogance, tribal loyalty over truth, and the futility of wealth without faith.
