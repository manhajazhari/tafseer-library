SURAH AT-TAHREEM · ٱلتَّحْرِيم

Prohibition — The Household, Repentance & Two Examples

Surah At-Tahreem is a Madani surah revealed about an incident within the Prophet's own home — the forbidding of a lawful thing to please his wives. From this private episode Allah draws eternal guidance: the expiation of oaths, the keeping of trusts and secrets, the call to sincere repentance (tawbah nasuh), the duty to guard yourself and your family from the Fire, firmness against disbelief and hypocrisy, and four unforgettable examples of women — the wives of Nuh and Lut, the wife of Pharaoh (Asiya), and Maryam — teaching that faith, not marriage or lineage, is what truly saves.
