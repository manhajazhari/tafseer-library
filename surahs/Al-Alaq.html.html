SURAH AL-ALAQ · ٱلْعَلَق

Read! — The First Word from Heaven

In a quiet cave on Mount Noor, far above the streets of Makkah, a forty-year-old man sat in contemplation. Suddenly, an angel appeared and delivered the most transformative command in human history: "Iqra'" — Read! With these words, the Quran began, prophethood was inaugurated, and the entire course of civilization changed forever. Surah Al-Alaq is the birth certificate of the final message.
