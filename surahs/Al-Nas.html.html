SURAH AN-NAS · ٱلنَّاس

An-Nas — Refuge from the Whisperer

Surah An-Nas seals the Quran. It teaches the believer to seek refuge in Allah — Rabb, Malik, and Ilah of mankind — from the whisperer who hides in the chest. The cure is built into the disease: Shaytan retreats the moment Allah is remembered. The Mushaf begins by asking guidance (Al-Fatiha) and closes by asking protection on the path (An-Nas).
