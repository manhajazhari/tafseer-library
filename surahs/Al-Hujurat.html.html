SURAH AL-HUJURAT · ٱلْحُجُرَات

The Victory — A Manifest Opening at Hudaybiyyah

Surah Al-Hujurat is a Madani surah of 18 verses, revealed in 9 AH — the Year of Delegations. As tribes streamed into Madinah to enter Islam, they brought desert manners with them. The surah laid down the inward and outward adab of the believing community: how to walk before Allah and His Messenger ﷺ, how to verify news, how to mediate conflict, how to guard the tongue from mockery and backbiting, how to see every human as a brother from one father and one mother — and how nobility is measured only by taqwa.
