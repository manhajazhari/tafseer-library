SURAH AL-MURSALAT · ٱلْمُرْسَلَات

Those Sent Forth — The Day of Decision Is Coming

Surah Al-Mursalat is a Makkan surah that opens with five mighty oaths — by the winds sent in succession, the storming gales, the spreading clouds, the separators of truth from falsehood, and the deliverers of the Reminder. The whole surah drives toward a single, unshakeable conclusion: what you are promised — the Day of Decision — will surely come. Ten times it strikes the heart with the refrain "Woe that Day to the deniers," painting the upheaval of the cosmos, the punishment of the wrongdoers, and the gardens, shades, and springs prepared for the God-conscious.
