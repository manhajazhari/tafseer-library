SURAH AL-NASR · ٱلنَّصْر

The Divine Victory — A Signal of the End and a Call to Glorify

After twenty-three years of struggle, persecution, migration, and battle, Allah revealed His final gift to the Prophet ﷺ: the news that the help of Allah had arrived, and that people would enter Islam in multitudes. Surah Al-Nasr is the shortest of the complete revelations about a world-changing event — and yet its command is not to celebrate, but to glorify Allah, praise Him, and seek His forgiveness. A lesson in humility at the moment of triumph.
