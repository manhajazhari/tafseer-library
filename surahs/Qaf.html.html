Qaf — The Surah of Resurrection and the Sharpened Sight

Surah Qaf is a Makkan surah of 45 verses, opening with the disjoined letter 'Qaf' and an oath by the Glorious Qur'an. It answers the disbelievers' astonishment that a human warner came to them and that dust could return as flesh. The surah parades the daily resurrections of the world — pillarless sky, anchoring mountains, blessed rain reviving dead land — then descends into the human chest: Allah is closer than the jugular vein, two angels record on the right and the left, the stupor of death will come, the Trumpet will be blown, and the earth will split in haste. It seals with a tender command to the Prophet ✌: 'you are not a tyrant — so remind by the Qur'an whoever fears My threat.'
