SURAH AT-TARIQ · ٱلطَّارِق

By the Night-Comer — A Piercing Star

In the deep silence of a Makkan night, Allah swears by the sky and by the brilliant star that pierces the darkness — "At-Tāriq" — to deliver three earth-shaking truths: every single soul has a guardian watching over it, man was created from a drop of fluid and will surely be brought back to life, and the Qur'an is a decisive word, not a joke. Surah At-Tariq is a thunderclap of certainty for a heedless world.
