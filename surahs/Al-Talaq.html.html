SURAH AT-TALAQ · ٱلطَّلَاق

Divorce — Justice, Taqwa & Allah's Provision

Surah At-Talaq is a Madani surah devoted to the rulings of divorce — its proper timing, the waiting period ('idda), housing, maintenance, and witnessing — all surrounded by justice and mercy. Yet its luminous heart is a universal promise repeated again and again: whoever fears Allah (taqwa), He makes for him a way out of every hardship, provides for him from where he does not expect, eases his affairs, effaces his sins, and magnifies his reward. The surah closes with a warning from the rebellious nations, the gift of the Reminder and the Messenger who leads from darkness into light, and the majesty of the Lord of the seven heavens.
