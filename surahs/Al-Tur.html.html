SURAH AT-TUR · ٱلطُّور

The Mount — Five Oaths, the Garden, and the Comfort of the Prophet ﷺ

Surah At-Tur is a Makkan surah of 49 verses, opening with five great oaths — the Mount, the Inscribed Book, the much-frequented House, the raised canopy, and the surging sea — and closing with the comfort of the Prophet ﷺ: 'be patient; you are in Our sight; glorify your Lord when you rise, in part of the night, and at the setting of the stars.'
