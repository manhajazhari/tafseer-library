SURAH AN-NABA · ٱلنَّبَأ

The Great News — The Day of Decision

Surah An-Naba is a powerful Makkan Surah that addresses the great news the disbelievers disputed about: the Resurrection and the Day of Judgment. Allah lists the wonders of His creation — the earth, mountains, sleep, day and night, the sky, the sun, the rains and gardens — as undeniable proofs of His power to raise the dead. Then it unveils the terrifying Day of Decision, the punishment of the rejecters in Hell, and the magnificent rewards awaiting the God-conscious in the Gardens of Bliss.
