SURAH AL-QADR · ٱلْقَدْر

The Night of Decree — A Night Better Than a Thousand Months

In the silence of one blessed night in Ramadan, the heavens opened and the Qur'an descended from the Preserved Tablet to the lowest sky. Surah Al-Qadr proclaims the majesty of that night — Laylat al-Qadr — a single night of worship worth more than a thousand months, a night when angels and the Spirit (Jibreel) descend with every decree, a night that is pure peace until the break of dawn. A celebration of the supreme gift Allah gave humanity: His final, eternal Book.
